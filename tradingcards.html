Powerspice Girls Trading Card Design

Assignment

Trading Card Design

Tools Used

Illustrator & Indesign

Year

2022

Artist trading cards (ATCs) are miniature pieces of art that are traded around the world. Artists create, trade and collect art at organized "swap" events, either in person or online.

I had to choose a theme, style and design a series of 3 (triptych) trading cards. I decided to do a Powerpuff Girls inspired trading cards. I put my own twist by making each character represent a common spice used in South Asian cuisine.

Specifications:

1. Card size: 2.5 x 3.5 inches.
2. Format: Portrait (Vertical).
3. Technique: Digital (If made by hand must be digitalized).
4. The front should feature a photo, design or illustration should be self-made.
