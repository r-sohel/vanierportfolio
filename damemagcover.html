DAME Magazine Cover

Assignment

Magazine Cover Layout

Tools Used

Indesign

Year

2024

I had to create a magazine layoutfor a publication and design a sample cover It had to include publication name, masthead (wordmark), cover image, dateline and coverlines, plugs, etc. keeping note of how all visual elements work in harmony with each other as well as the established brand.

The magazine cover I designed targets modern, fashion-conscious women who are passionate about high-end fashion, beauty, and culture. These readers are likely professionals or trendsetters seeking the latest runway trends, elegant style guides, and beauty tips.

The choice of bold yet refined typography, combined with a sophisticated color palette, speaks directly to the target audience’s desire for a luxurious and exclusive experience. The cover emphasizes elegance and innovation, reflecting the magazine’s focus on haute couture makeup techniques and exclusive industry insights. By incorporating elements of runway fashion, exclusive interviews, and royal collections, the design aligns with the aspirational and high-fashion voice of the publication, ensuring it resonates with the target audience.
