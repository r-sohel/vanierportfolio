Assignment

Social Justice POster

Tools Used

Photoshop

Year

2022

This assignment's requirement was to find a musician / artist that I liked and find 3 - 5 of their songs to include in my original design of a CD booklet with lyrics for a Single or EP*

*An EP (Extended Play) refers to a half-length body of work, features between 4-6 tracks and has a running time of roughly 15-22 mins (but can be up to 30 minutes). A single is the shortest body of work you can release, features between 1-3 tracks and has a running time of roughly less than 10 mins.

Pages:

1 main cover : artist logo, Title of the Single/EP, artwork and/or Band photography 2 - 3 (inside spread) : Lyrics, photos and/or artwork ... 4 back of booklet : Song credits, track list, artwork credits & legal info PLUS artwork and/or photography

I had to create a final PDF for both print (via Indesign) and digital contribution.

I kept the PSD file for an additional part 2 of this project which was to animate the CD Cover using Photoshop's Timeline
